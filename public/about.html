About GEEOOH Hotel

Luxury, Comfort, and Exceptional Service Since 2024

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Geeooh Hotel has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality in Beverly Hills. Our commitment to excellence and personalized service has made us a favorite among discerning travelers from around the world.

Every detail of our hotel has been carefully designed to provide an unforgettable experience. From our elegantly appointed rooms to our world-class amenities, we ensure that every guest feels like royalty.

Located in the heart of Beverly Hills, we offer easy access to the city's finest shopping, dining, and entertainment. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, Geeooh Hotel provides the perfect home away from home.
